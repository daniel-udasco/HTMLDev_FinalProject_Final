Enrollment Instructions

𝐊𝐢𝐧𝐝𝐥𝐲 𝐟𝐨𝐥𝐥𝐨𝐰 𝐭𝐡𝐞 𝐬𝐭𝐞𝐩𝐬 𝐚𝐧𝐝 𝐫𝐞𝐚𝐝 𝐭𝐡𝐞 𝐢𝐧𝐬𝐭𝐫𝐮𝐜𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧𝐬 𝐜𝐚𝐫𝐞𝐟𝐮𝐥𝐥𝐲:

🔴𝐒𝐓𝐄𝐏 𝟏:
Scan the QR code or click ers.tup.edu.ph/aims/applicants/ and click "Apply."

🔴𝐒𝐓𝐄𝐏 𝟐:
Complete the Profile Verification Form:
- Select "Visayas Campus" and click "Continue."
- Fill out all required information.
➡️Ensure you complete the Family Background and Educational Attainment sections.
➡️Upload a low-resolution 2x2 image in .jpeg or .jpg format.
➡️Skip the Employment Background section but check the "Data Subject Consent Form."

🔴𝐒𝐓𝐄𝐏 𝟑:
Click the "Exam Schedule" button at the top of the page and choose March 22, 2025.

🔴𝐒𝐓𝐄𝐏 𝟒:
THIS IS VERY IMPORTANT: Save a screenshot or print the application form containing:
- USERNAME (Applicant ID number)
- PASSWORD
- Proof of exam date (March 22, 2025) and location (TUPV Talisay)
Present this to admission personnel on exam day.

𝐒𝐓𝐄𝐏 𝟓:
Fill out the Admission Google Form: https://forms.gle/uxxEvzLqu38vFB539

On the Day of the Examination:
-Present the screenshot or printed copy
-Bring two sharpened pencils
-Dress appropriately: No shorts, sleeveless tops, or slippers
-Arrive 30 minutes before the scheduled exam
